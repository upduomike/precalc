AP Precalculus Practice Test

Multiple Choice Questions

1. Given \( f(x) = x^2 \), which of the following represents \( g(x) = (x - 3)^2 + 2 \)?

A) A horizontal shift left by 3 and an upward shift by 2
B) A horizontal shift right by 3 and a downward shift by 2
C) A horizontal shift right by 3 and an upward shift by 2
D) A horizontal shift left by 3 and a downward shift by 2

2. Which of the following is equivalent to \( 1 - 2\sin^2(x) \)?

A) \( \cos(2x) \)
B) \( \sin(2x) \)
C) \( 2\cos^2(x) - 1 \)
D) \( \tan(2x) \)

3. The population of a town doubles every 5 years. If the current population is 1,200, which function models the population \( P(t) \) after \( t \) years?

A) \( P(t) = 1200 \cdot 2^t \)
B) \( P(t) = 1200 \cdot 2^{t/5} \)
C) \( P(t) = 1200 \cdot e^{0.5t} \)
D) \( P(t) = 1200 \cdot (1 + 0.5)^t \)

4. Consider the function \( f(x) = x^3 - 3x^2 - 4x + 12 \). Which of the following describes the end behavior of \( f(x) \)?

A) As \( x \to \infty, f(x) \to \infty \); as \( x \to -\infty, f(x) \to -\infty \)
B) As \( x \to \infty, f(x) \to -\infty \); as \( x \to -\infty, f(x) \to \infty \)
C) As \( x \to \infty, f(x) \to -\infty \); as \( x \to -\infty, f(x) \to -\infty \)
D) As \( x \to \infty, f(x) \to \infty \); as \( x \to -\infty, f(x) \to \infty \)

Free Response Questions

5. Sketch the graph of \( y = 2\cos\left(\frac{\pi}{2}x\right) \) for \( x \) in the interval \( [0, 4] \). Describe the key points.



6. Solve for \( x \): \( 2 \cdot 3^x = 54 \). Show all steps.



7. Given \( f(x) = x^3 - 6x^2 + 11x - 6 \), find all the zeros of the polynomial. Confirm your answers by using synthetic division or another method.



8. Find the sum of the first 8 terms of the arithmetic sequence where the first term is 5 and the common difference is 3.



9. Evaluate the limit: \( \lim_{x \to 2} \frac{x^2 - 4}{x - 2} \). Explain the process and determine if the function is continuous at \( x = 2 \).



10. A projectile is launched from the ground with an initial velocity of 80 feet per second. Its height after \( t \) seconds is given by \( h(t) = -16t^2 + 80t \).
a) Find the time when the projectile reaches its maximum height.
b) What is the maximum height reached by the projectile?
